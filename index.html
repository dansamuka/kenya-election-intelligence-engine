    Beyond the horse race

    Candidate percentages are one signal among several. These are usually more decision-relevant to how 2027 actually unfolds: who allies with whom, whether people trust the process at all, and whether the current governing coalition holds together.

    Coalition & alliance preference

    Election integrity confidence

    A different question from candidate preference: do people trust the process at all.

    Broad-Based Government support

    Poll verification ledger

    Kenya's 2027 cycle will see fabricated "poll results" circulate. This is a public, growing record of specific claims checked against real evidence — not an exhaustive fact-check service, just what's been verified here so far.

    Coalition scenario lab

    Transfer efficiency controls how much of each member candidate's individual support carries over when they merge into a coalition. 100% assumes perfect, frictionless vote transfer — a real coalition typically leaks some support to backlash, competing loyalties, or turnout effects, so try lowering this to stress-test that assumption.
